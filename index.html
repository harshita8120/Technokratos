About Us

Technokratos, the much-anticipated annual gala organized by the CSSE department at MMMUT Gorakhpur, turns campus into a whirlwind of creativity and camaraderie. Between coding marathons and tech workshops, the stage erupts with high-energy dance battles and soulful singing contests that let every performer captivate the crowd. A dazzling fashion show follows, where budding designers and models strut bold, original looks under the spotlight. Meanwhile, freestyle drawing competitions invite artists to transform blank canvases into vibrant masterpieces against the clock. With laughter echoing through open-mic sessions and impromptu jam circles springing up in every corner, Technokratos isn’t just an event—it’s a celebration of talent, teamwork, and unfiltered fun that leaves everyone buzzing long after the lights go down.

The CSSE Society at MMMUT Gorakhpur is the beating heart of every tech-savvy student’s college experience, bringing together programmers, designers, and innovators under one collaborative roof. Through regular coding sessions and peer-led workshops, the society demystifies everything from algorithmic problem-solving to full-stack app development. It sponsors hackathons that spark friendly competition, invites industry experts for guest lectures that bridge classroom theory with real-world practice, and organizes project showcases where members present their latest creations. Beyond the screens, the group cultivates a strong sense of community with mentorship programs, open-mic tech talks and networking socials—ensuring that every member grows not just as a coder, but as a confident, connected contributor to the ever-evolving world of computer science.
